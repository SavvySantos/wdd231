Site Name (Tacos Bravo)

This website will help us establish a professional online presence as we transition from a canopy setup to a full food trailer. It will give customers a convenient place to find our menu, location, events we will be at, and contact information. We'll also showcase photos of our dishes so visitors can see just how delicious our food is!

Scenarios

  • What events are coming up that Tacos Bravo will be attending?
  • What are the specials for this month?
  • How can I book Tacos Bravo for a private event I have coming up?
  • Can I make an online order?
  • When we aren't at an event, where will we be?
